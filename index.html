I interned at Intel Labs, Hillsboro in Fall 2018. During my internship, I focused on the secure multi-tenancy in FPGAs.

Before starting my PhD, I was a graduate researcher in the Design-for-Test department at Intel Deutschland GmbH, Munich between March 2013 and July 2016. I completed my MSc at Technical University of Darmstadt in 2012 and BE at University of Mumbai in 2006.
